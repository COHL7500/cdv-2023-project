CDV - The bigger picture

Picture a world where the animals we're used to are missing. No bees buzzing around, no honey to taste, flowers unable to bloom, and no birds soaring in the air. If the Earth keeps getting warmer, this could actually happen sooner than we think.

"The bigger picture" is a project that takes you on an adventure to learn how different animals are affected by changes in temperature.
